About Plant-Based Buds

Plant-Based Buds was created as a FaceBook group in 2017 to help people share and discover vegan recipes and to discuss different aspects of living a cruelty-free, more environmentally sustainable lifestyle. The purpose of this site is to provide a place where you can easily search by nutrient (macro, vitamin, or mineral) to find top plant-based sources, with a focus on whole, unproccessed foods.
